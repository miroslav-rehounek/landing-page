About

With 6+ years across cloud engineering and architecture, I've worked with AWS and Azure in contexts ranging from greenfield platform builds to security-critical enterprise migrations. My career arc has moved from hands-on software development through platform and DevOps engineering to solution architecture and presales — giving me a practical end-to-end perspective on how systems are built, operated, and justified to stakeholders.

I hold a dual degree — Computer Science at FIT CTU Prague and Law at Charles University — a combination that directly shapes how I approach risk, governance, and compliance. I treat regulatory and contractual constraints as design inputs, not afterthoughts, which tends to produce architectures that hold up under scrutiny.
